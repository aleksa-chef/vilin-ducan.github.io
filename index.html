Material

the material I use consists of tools that are used by most artists and that are widely known to anyone involved in this type of art. I can often insert some of my inventions to beautify my work. I try to work the old-school using brushes, glue and paint, I also use cotton, wood and mold for plaster work.

Retro Style

Retro style is something that attracts me most to decoupage and I try to create collections that will always represent and express that old-fashioned, ethno experience and that will let the customer know through them the abstract nature of true retro style.
